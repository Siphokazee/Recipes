Classic California Roll

About California Roll

The California Roll is a popular sushi roll that offers a delightful combination of flavors and textures. It's known for its simplicity and great taste.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Imitation crab or real crab
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Lay a bamboo sushi rolling mat on a clean surface.
  2. Place a sheet of nori on the mat.
  3. Wet your hands and spread a layer of sushi rice evenly on the nori, leaving a small border at the top.
  4. Add thin strips of crab, avocado, and cucumber to the center of the rice.
  5. Roll the nori tightly using the mat, sealing the roll with a bit of water.
  6. Sprinkle sesame seeds on the outside of the roll for added flavor and texture.
  7. Cut the California Roll into bite-sized pieces.
  8. Serve with soy sauce and wasabi.
