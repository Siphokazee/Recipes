Rainbow Roll

About Rainbow roll

The Rainbow Sushi Roll is a visually stunning and delicious sushi creation that combines various types of seafood to create a colorful masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Ahi tuna
  • Salmon
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Imitation crab or real crab

Steps

  1. Lay a bamboo sushi rolling mat on a clean surface.
  2. Place a sheet of nori on the mat.
  3. Wet your hands and spread a layer of sushi rice evenly on the nori, leaving a small border at the top.
  4. Add thin strips of ahi tuna, salmon, avocado, cucumber, and crab to the center of the rice.
  5. Roll the nori tightly using the mat, sealing the roll with a bit of water.
  6. Cut the Rainbow Sushi Roll into bite-sized pieces.
  7. Arrange the sushi pieces on a plate, alternating between ahi tuna, salmon, and avocado to create a colorful pattern.
  8. Serve with soy sauce and wasabi.
